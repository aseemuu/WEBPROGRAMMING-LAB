Department of computer application

A computer application (or simply application or app) is a type of software designed to help users perform specific tasks or functions on a computer or digital device. These tasks can range from word processing, spreadsheet management, and presentations to more complex activities like photo editing, accounting, and database management. Computer applications can be installed locally on a device or accessed via the internet (as web applications). They are commonly used in business, education, healthcare, entertainment, and many other fields to increase productivity and efficiency.
